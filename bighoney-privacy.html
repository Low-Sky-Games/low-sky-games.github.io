Privacy Policy — Big Honey

Last updated: 2026-07-23.

Big Honey ("the game") is developed by Low Sky Games ("we"). This policy explains what data the game handles.

What we collect

Nothing, ourselves. The game has no accounts, no sign-in, no server of our own, and no behavioral analytics. Your save (game progress and settings) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app deletes it.

Save backups

On iOS, the game can keep a backup copy of your save in your own iCloud account (the app's private iCloud container) so progress survives reinstalling or changing devices. That data is yours, stored under your Apple ID; we cannot read it. On Android, saves are included in Android's standard Auto Backup to your Google Drive, under your Google account, with the same property: we never see it.

Third-party services

The game uses two third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  1. 1. Google AdMob (advertising) — shows optional, rewarded ads only: an ad plays only when you explicitly choose to watch one for an in-game bonus. There are no forced or interstitial ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's privacy policy. In the EEA/UK, a consent dialog (Google UMP) is shown before any ad personalization; you can choose non-personalized ads. On iOS, the App Tracking Transparency prompt lets you refuse tracking entirely — the game works identically either way.
  2. 2. RevenueCat (purchase management) — if you buy the optional "Big Honey: Chill" unlock, RevenueCat processes the purchase receipt issued by Apple/Google to keep your purchase restorable across devices, per the RevenueCat privacy policy. Payment itself is handled entirely by Apple or Google; we never see payment details.

Crash reports

If a future version enables crash reporting (Sentry), it would receive technical crash data only — stack traces, device model, OS version — to help us fix bugs; never your save contents or an advertising identifier. The current version sends no crash reports beyond what Apple/Google collect with your OS-level consent.

Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. Ads are configured for a general audience.

Offline play

The game is fully playable offline. With no network connection, no data leaves your device at all.

Changes & contact

We will update this page if the game's data practices change. Questions: support@lowsky.games.
